Absinthine Jewelry : About

Absinthine

Jewelry by Michaelle Pearson
Old Lyme, CT

Absinthine Jewelry is inspired by the Alchemical process:
The transformative nature of Fire.
The elevation of element to Art.
The subtle skills honed by time and patience—knowing when the metal is ready to sing its true song.


Michaelle Pearson studied silversmithing, goldsmithing, and enameling at the School of Visual Arts, and is a member of the Lyme Artisans Guild.

Absinthine Jewelry is available at the Florence Griswold Museum Shop, select coastal Connecticut galleries, and at Lyme Artisans Guild events.
